# Weeks 1–5 — Day-by-Day Plans

> Each day follows the **daily rhythm** from the README: Morning Meeting → Reading & Phonics → Brain Break → Math → Writing/Journal → Theme/Project → Read-aloud. Times are targets, not rules. Weekends are light (a read-aloud + one game is plenty).
> **Legend:** 🟦 = 2nd-grade stretch (offer if she's ready) · 📚 = book basket · 🎯 = weekly checkpoint

---

## WEEK 1 — 🎒 Ready, Set, Summer! (All About Me)

**Big idea:** Establish the routine, get a baseline, and make her feel like a capable "Academy student." Low pressure, high warmth.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Review all 5 short vowels; blend & read CVC words confidently.
- **Sight words:** the, and, a, to, I, see, we, can *(review — see `sight-words.md`)*.
- **Writing:** Write her name neatly; write one word per picture (labeling).
- **Math:** Count to 100; read/write numbers 0–20; compare (more/less/equal).
- **Do the [Baseline check](./assessment-checklists.md) this week** and adjust everything else accordingly.

**📚 Book basket:** *The Kissing Hand* · *Chrysanthemum* · *The Name Jar* · *Pete the Cat: Rocking in My School Shoes* · any "all about me" picture book.

| Day | Reading & Phonics (20m) | Math (20m) | Journal / Writing (10m) | Theme & Read-aloud (20m) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Letter-sound speed review (flash all 26). Sort objects by beginning sound. | Count aloud to 50; write numbers 0–10 on whiteboard. | Write her full name 3×; decorate it. | Start "All About Me" book (cover: self-portrait). Read-aloud: *The Kissing Hand*. |
| **Tue** | Short **a** family: cat, hat, map, bag. Build words with letter tiles. | Count to 100 by 1s (point to a 100-chart). Find missing numbers. | "All About Me" page: *My family* (draw + label names). | *Chrysanthemum*; count letters in everyone's name (graph it). |
| **Wed** | Short **e / i**: bed, pen / pig, win. Blend & read on whiteboard. | Skip-count by **10s** to 100; clap each ten. | Page: *My favorite food* (draw + label). | *The Name Jar*. Talk: what makes you *you*? |
| **Thu** | Short **o / u**: dog, mop / sun, bug. Read 6 CVC words in a row. | Compare numbers: **more / less / equal** with bean piles. | Page: *I am good at…* (draw + try a sentence). | Pete the Cat read-aloud; sing the song. |
| **Fri** | **Baseline phonics check** (game-style). Reread a BOB Book #1. | **Baseline math check** + number-line hop game. | Finish & "publish" the All About Me book; read it to a stuffed animal. | Free choice from the basket. Celebrate week 1! 🎉 |

**Featured project:** *All About Me* book — becomes page 1 of her Academy Portfolio.
🎯 **Checkpoint:** complete the baseline; note her true starting point.

## WEEK 2 — 🌊 Under the Sea

**Big idea:** Dive into **digraphs** (two letters, one sound) and **place value** (tens & ones) — both are "things group together."
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read words with **sh, ch, th, wh, ck**.
- **Sight words:** like, he, she, was, for, you, they.
- **Writing:** Write a word with a digraph; start spacing words.
- **Math:** Understand a 2-digit number as **tens + ones**; build, compare, and write them.

**📚 Book basket:** *The Rainbow Fish* · *Pout-Pout Fish* · *Commotion in the Ocean* · *Hello Ocean* · *Fish Is Fish*.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **sh**: ship, fish, shell, wish. Underline the *sh*. | Bundle 10 straws/sticks = "1 ten." Build 23, 31. | Draw a fish; label "fish," "shell." | *The Rainbow Fish*; talk about sharing. |
| **Tue** | **ch**: chip, chin, lunch, beach. Sort sh/ch pictures. | Show numbers with ten-frames + base-ten blocks. | Sentence frame: "I see a ___." (sea animal). | *Pout-Pout Fish*; make grumpy/happy faces. |
| **Wed** | **th**: this, that, bath, with. (voiced vs unvoiced — just notice it). | "How many tens? how many ones?" for 45, 60, 18. | Label an ocean drawing with 3 words. | *Commotion in the Ocean*; act out animals. |
| **Thu** | **wh / ck**: whale, when / duck, rock, sock. | Compare 2-digit numbers with >, <, = (alligator mouth). | Write: "The whale is big." (capital + period). | Ocean-in-a-bottle craft (water + oil + blue). |
| **Fri** | Digraph review; read a decodable with sh/ch/th. | 🟦 Expanded form: 24 = 20 + 4. Build & say. | Add a page to portfolio: ocean facts. | Finish ocean bottle; "swim" brain-break dance. |

**Featured project:** Ocean-in-a-bottle + a "reef" built from tens/ones blocks (math you can see).
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she tell you the tens and ones in a number like 37?

## WEEK 3 — 🐛 Bugs & Butterflies

**Big idea:** **Beginning blends** (two sounds you *do* hear) and **addition** as "putting together." Theme: life cycles & symmetry.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read **l-blends, r-blends, s-blends** (bl, cl, gr, tr, st, sn…).
- **Sight words:** have, this, with, are, on, but, all.
- **Writing:** Write a sentence with finger spaces between words.
- **Math:** Add within 10, then within 20, using **count-on** and **make-ten** strategies.

**📚 Book basket:** *The Very Hungry Caterpillar* · *The Very Busy Spider* · *Bugs! Bugs! Bugs!* · *Waiting for Wings* · *Some Bugs*.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **l-blends**: bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl (blob, clip, flag). | Addition = combining. Add with bug counters within 6. | Draw a bug; label body parts (3 labels). | *The Very Hungry Caterpillar*; sequence the days. |
| **Tue** | **r-blends**: br, cr, dr, gr, tr (crab, drum, grin). | **Count-on** strategy: 7 + 2 → "sevennn… 8, 9." | Sentence: "The bug can ___." | *The Very Busy Spider*; feel the raised web. |
| **Wed** | **s-blends**: sc, sk, sn, sp, st, sw (stop, snip, swim). | **Make-ten**: 8 + 5 → 8+2=10, +3 = 13 (ten-frames). | Write 2 sentences about a bug. | Start butterfly life-cycle wheel (egg→larva→pupa→adult). |
| **Thu** | Blends review; read a blends decodable. | Add within 20 with ten-frames; record number sentences. | Journal: "If I were a bug I would…" | Bug hunt outside; tally what you find. |
| **Fri** | Blend ladder game (change one sound: stop→step→stem). | Addition word problems with bugs (act them out). | Finish butterfly wheel; label the stages. | Symmetry art: paint half a butterfly, fold. |

**Featured project:** Butterfly life-cycle wheel + a real bug hunt with a tally chart.
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she add 8 + 6 using make-ten (not just guessing)?

## WEEK 4 — 🚀 Blast Off! (Space)

**Big idea:** **Ending blends** and **subtraction** as "taking away / finding the difference." Countdown = built-in subtraction fun.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read **ending blends**: -nd, -nt, -st, -sk, -mp, -lt, -nk, -ng.
- **Sight words:** said, what, out, up, look, go, come.
- **Writing:** Write a sentence and read it back; check for a capital & period.
- **Math:** Subtract within 10 → 20 with **count-back** and **fact families**.

**📚 Book basket:** *There's No Place Like Space* (Cat in the Hat) · *Mae Among the Stars* · *Roaring Rockets* · *Papa, Please Get the Moon for Me* · *The Darkest Dark*.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **-nd / -nt**: hand, sand / tent, hunt. | Subtraction = take away. "5 rockets, 2 fly off…" | Draw a rocket; label 3 parts. | *Mae Among the Stars*; dream big. |
| **Tue** | **-st / -sk**: nest, fast / mask, desk. | **Count-back**: 9 − 2 → "ninnne… 8, 7." | Sentence: "I see a ___ in space." | *There's No Place Like Space*; name planets. |
| **Wed** | **-mp / -lt**: jump, lamp / belt, melt. | **Fact families**: 3+4=7, 4+3=7, 7−3=4, 7−4=3. | Write 2 sentences about an astronaut. | Build paper/straw rocket. |
| **Thu** | **-nk / -ng**: bank, sink / ring, king, song. | Subtract within 20; relate to addition. | Journal: "On the moon I would…" | Countdown 10→0 (subtraction!); "launch" the rocket. |
| **Fri** | Ending-blend review; read a decodable. | Mixed +/− word problems; ten-frame check. | Add a "space facts" page to portfolio. | Constellation art (black paper + star stickers). |

**Featured project:** Design, build, and launch a paper rocket; a 10→0 countdown that *is* subtraction.
🎯 **Checkpoint:** does she see that 7 − 3 and 3 + 4 are related (fact families)?

## WEEK 5 — 🌱 Growing Garden  *(Mid-Summer Check)*

**Big idea:** **Silent-e** magic (the vowel "says its name") and **fact fluency**. Plant something real and track its growth — a living math/science journal.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read **silent-e / long vowels**: a_e, i_e, o_e, u_e, e_e; contrast with CVC (cap/cape, kit/kite).
- **Sight words:** review weeks 1–4 + new: little, do, did, yes, no.
- **Writing:** Write 2 related sentences (a beginning and a next).
- **Math:** **Fluency** with +/− within 10; doubles & near-doubles; missing addend; word problems.
- **Run the [Mid-summer check](./assessment-checklists.md) on Friday.**

**📚 Book basket:** *The Tiny Seed* · *Lola Plants a Garden* · *Planting a Rainbow* · *Jack and the Beanstalk* · *From Seed to Plant*.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **a_e**: cake, name, gate. Contrast cap → cape. | **Doubles**: 4+4, 5+5… (visual). Speed game to 10. | Plant seeds in a cup; draw + label "seed, soil." | *The Tiny Seed*; predict what happens. |
| **Tue** | **i_e**: bike, time, kite. Contrast kit → kite. | **Near-doubles**: 5+6 = (5+5)+1. | Growth journal Day 1: draw the cup, write the date. | *Lola Plants a Garden*; plan a garden. |
| **Wed** | **o_e / u_e**: home, nose / cube, cute. | **Missing addend**: 3 + ⬜ = 8 (use fingers/objects). | Write 2 sentences: "I planted a seed. It needs ___." | *Planting a Rainbow*; sort colors. |
| **Thu** | **e_e** + mixed silent-e read; decodable book. | +/− within 10 word problems; record equations. | Growth journal update; measure the sprout 🟦 in cm. | Parts of a plant (roots/stem/leaf/flower) diagram. |
| **Fri** | **Mid-summer phonics check** (game). Reread a favorite. | **Mid-summer math check** + a fluency card game. | Celebrate the halfway portfolio review together. | Free read; talk about how much she's grown — like the plant! 🌱 |

**Featured project:** Plant seeds + a daily **growth journal** (date, drawing, measurement) running through Week 10.
🎯 **Checkpoint:** **Mid-summer assessment.** Compare to baseline; pick any skill to reinforce in weeks 6–10. Re-teach without hesitation if silent-e or addition fluency isn't solid.
