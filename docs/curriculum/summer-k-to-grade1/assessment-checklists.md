# Assessment & Progress Checklists

These are **show-me games, not tests.** Sit together, keep it light ("Let's see what you can do — this helps me pick the fun stuff!"), and stop if she tires. The point is to (1) find her real starting line, (2) catch anything to reinforce, and (3) let her *see her own growth*.

**How to record:** print this page (or copy the boxes), and mark each item on three dates:
`✅ solid` · `🔶 emerging` · `⬜ not yet`. Re-mark the same sheet at Baseline (Wk 1), Mid (Wk 5), and Final (Wk 10) in three colors so growth is visible at a glance.

---

## A. Baseline Placement Check — do this in Week 1

Keep each section to a few minutes. **Use the results to adjust:** lots of ✅ → pull stretch goals forward; several ⬜ → slow down and reinforce fundamentals first.

### Reading & Phonics
- ⬜ Names all 26 **letters** (upper & lower)
- ⬜ Gives the **sound** for each letter
- ⬜ Hears & says **rhymes** (cat/hat); claps **syllables** (ba‑na‑na)
- ⬜ Blends sounds into a **CVC word** (/c/ /a/ /t/ → "cat")
- ⬜ Reads 5 CVC words: `sat, pin, log, bed, run`
- ⬜ Reads digraph words: `ship, chin, that` *(checks if Week 2 is review or new)*
- ⬜ Sight words — check the **Pre-Primer & Primer** lists (`sight-words.md`); count how many she reads instantly: ______ / 92

### Number Sense & Math
- ⬜ Counts aloud to: ______ (target ≥ 30; many reach 100)
- ⬜ Counts **10 objects** accurately (1-to-1)
- ⬜ Writes numbers 0–10 (also try 0–20: ______)
- ⬜ Identifies which group is **more / less**
- ⬜ Adds within 5 with objects (3 + 2)
- ⬜ 🔶 Adds within 10; 🔶 understands take-away (subtraction)
- ⬜ 🔶 Knows **tens & ones** idea at all (most won't yet — that's Week 2)

### Writing & Fine Motor
- ⬜ Writes her **first name** legibly
- ⬜ Holds pencil with a workable grip
- ⬜ Draws a person with several parts
- ⬜ 🔶 Writes any word other than her name

**Baseline summary (one line you'll love re-reading in August):**
> On ____ /____ /2026, she can: _______________________________________________

---

## B. Mid-Summer Check — Week 5 (Friday)

Celebrate first, then check. Re-teach anything ⬜ during weeks 6–10 (it's fine to spend a few extra days).

### Reading & Phonics
- ⬜ Reads all **digraphs** (sh, ch, th, wh, ck)
- ⬜ Reads **beginning & ending blends** (stop, jump, hand, nest)
- ⬜ Reads **silent-e** words (cake, bike, home) and contrasts cap/cape
- ⬜ Reads a **decodable book** mostly independently
- ⬜ **Retells** what happened (beginning → middle → end)
- ⬜ Sight words: reads ______ new words from weeks 1–5

### Math
- ⬜ Counts to **100**; reads/writes numbers to 100
- ⬜ Tells **tens & ones** in a 2-digit number (37 = 3 tens, 7 ones)
- ⬜ Compares 2-digit numbers (>, <, =)
- ⬜ **Adds within 20** using count-on / make-ten
- ⬜ **Subtracts within 20**; sees **fact families**
- ⬜ 🔶 Recalls some +/− facts within 10 quickly (fluency building)

### Writing
- ⬜ Writes a **complete sentence** (capital, finger spaces, period)
- ⬜ Writes **two related sentences**

---

## C. Final Check — Week 10 (the big celebration 🎓)

Compare against the same sheet from Week 1. The leap is usually dramatic — show her!

### ✅ "1st-Grade Ready" — the target (she should mostly hit these)
**Reading & Language**
- ⬜ Knows every letter sound incl. digraphs
- ⬜ Decodes CVC, blends, silent-e, and **vowel-team** words
- ⬜ Reads **~100+ sight words** instantly
- ⬜ Reads simple books and **retells the story**
- ⬜ Answers "who / what / where / why" about a read-aloud
- ⬜ Writes a **clear sentence**; attempts 2–3 sentence stories
**Math**
- ⬜ Counts to **120**; reads/writes numbers to 100
- ⬜ **Adds & subtracts within 20** (fluent within 10)
- ⬜ Understands **place value** (tens & ones)
- ⬜ Tells time to the **hour & half-hour**
- ⬜ Names 2D/3D **shapes**; splits shapes into **halves & fourths**
- ⬜ Measures length with a ruler

### 🟦 "2nd-Grade Stretch" — bonus wins (celebrate any of these!)
- ⬜ Reads **r-controlled** & **diphthong** words (bird, coin, cloud)
- ⬜ Reads with **expression**; starts **early chapter books**
- ⬜ Reads into the **Grade-2 sight-word list**
- ⬜ Writes a **3–5 sentence story** with beginning/middle/end
- ⬜ **Two-digit addition** (24 + 13)
- ⬜ Tells time to **5 minutes**; **counts coins**
- ⬜ **Skip-counts** by 2s, 5s, 10s; builds **arrays** ("3 rows of 4")

---

## D. Interpreting results & next steps
- **Mostly ✅ on "ready," several 🟦 stretch:** Outstanding — she's entering 1st grade ahead. Start a **Program 02** (fluency / add-subtract to 100 / story writing) for light maintenance.
- **Mostly ✅ on "ready," few 🟦:** Exactly on target. Keep the nightly read-aloud + 2–3 short sessions/week into the school year.
- **Several 🔶/⬜ on "ready":** Totally fine. Pick the **2–3 highest-leverage skills** (usually: phonics decoding, sight words, addition/subtraction within 20) and keep practicing those specifically. Mastery beats the calendar.

## E. Make it official 🎓
Create a **"Kaelyn's Academy — Summer Bridge" certificate** with her name, the date, and 3 things she's proud of. Take a photo of the Week-1 baseline page next to the Week-10 page. Kids are genuinely moved seeing how far they came — it's the best motivation for the next program.

---

*These checklists are designed to become the platform's **skill rubric**: each item maps to a skill tag (e.g. `phonics.silent-e`, `math.place-value`, `writing.sentence`) and a `ready` / `stretch` band. The agentic tutor will set each skill's state from check results and recommend the next activity or program accordingly.*
