# Weeks 6–10 — Day-by-Day Plans

> Same daily rhythm as weeks 1–5. These weeks reach harder into **2nd-grade stretch** (🟦) territory: vowel teams, r-controlled vowels, two-digit math, time & money, geometry, and skip-counting toward multiplication. Keep it joyful; offer the stretch, never force it.
> **Legend:** 🟦 = 2nd-grade stretch · 📚 = book basket · 🎯 = weekly checkpoint

---

## WEEK 6 — 🦕 Dinosaurs & Long Ago

**Big idea:** **Vowel teams** (two vowels walking, the first does the talking) and **two-digit addition** — big numbers feel as exciting as big dinosaurs.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read vowel teams **ai, ay, ee, ea, oa, ow** (rain, play, feet, leaf, boat, snow).
- **Sight words:** *(Grade 1)* when, your, can, from, good, any, how.
- **Writing:** Write a 3-sentence "story" with a beginning, middle, end.
- **Math:** 🟦 **Two-digit addition without regrouping** (24 + 13) using tens & ones; intro to lining up place value.

**📚 Book basket:** *Dinosaurs Before Dark* (Magic Tree House #1) · *How Do Dinosaurs Say Goodnight?* · *Dinosaur Roar!* · *The Magic School Bus: In the Time of Dinosaurs* · a dinosaur fact book.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **ai / ay**: rain, tail / play, day. Sort by spelling. | Review tens & ones; build two 2-digit numbers. | Story start: "Once there was a dinosaur…" | Begin *Dinosaurs Before Dark* (chapter book read-aloud!). |
| **Tue** | **ee / ea**: feet, see / leaf, eat. Both say long-e. | 🟦 Add tens to tens, ones to ones: 24 + 13 (blocks). | Story middle: "Then…" (2 sentences). | Dino "dig": hide toy dinos/bones in sand or rice. |
| **Wed** | **oa / ow**: boat, coat / snow, bowl. | 🟦 Two-digit addition no-regroup, written form (stack them). | Story end: "Finally…" Read the whole story aloud. | Measure dinosaur footprints (which is longest?). |
| **Thu** | Vowel-team mixed read; decodable/leveled book. | Add three small numbers; check with objects. | Illustrate the 3-sentence story; add to portfolio. | Continue chapter book; predict the next chapter. |
| **Fri** | Vowel-team review game ("teams" sorting race). | 🟦 Word problems with two-digit addition. | Free journal: favorite dinosaur + why (2–3 sentences). | Make a fossil (salt-dough imprint) or dino diorama. |

**Featured project:** Dinosaur "dig" + fossil-making + measuring footprints (length preview for Week 7).
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she read 5 vowel-team words and add 24 + 13 with blocks?

## WEEK 7 — ⛅ Weather & Seasons

**Big idea:** **R-controlled vowels** (the *r* "bosses" the vowel) and **measurement** — measure the weather, measure everything.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read **ar, or, er, ir, ur** (car, fork, her, bird, turn).
- **Sight words:** *(Grade 1)* every, after, think, around, again, by, could.
- **Writing:** Write about a sequence (first / next / last) — a weather report.
- **Math:** Measure & compare **length** (nonstandard units → 🟦 inches/cm with a ruler); estimate then measure.

**📚 Book basket:** *The Snowy Day* · *Cloudy With a Chance of Meatballs* · *Tap Tap Boom Boom* · *Rain* · *What Will the Weather Be?*

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **ar**: car, star, farm. | Measure with cubes/paperclips: "How many long?" | Start a daily **weather chart** (sun/cloud/rain icons). | *The Snowy Day*; talk about seasons. |
| **Tue** | **or**: fork, corn, storm. | Estimate, then measure 3 objects. Compare longest/shortest. | "Today's weather is ___ because ___." | Make a **rain gauge** (bottle + ruler). |
| **Wed** | **er**: her, fern, water. | 🟦 Measure with a **ruler** (inches); read the numbers. | Write a 3-step weather report (First/Next/Last). | *Cloudy With a Chance of Meatballs*; invent silly weather. |
| **Thu** | **ir / ur**: bird, girl / turn, fur. | 🟦 Compare lengths: "The pencil is 2 inches longer." | Growth-journal check: measure the plant in inches. | Cloud types; spot shapes in the sky. |
| **Fri** | R-controlled review; decodable read. | Measurement scavenger hunt (find something ~6 inches). | Portfolio: best weather report page. | Water cycle in a bag (tape to window, watch). |

**Featured project:** A working rain gauge + a week-long weather chart (data she collects daily).
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she use a ruler to measure to the nearest inch?

## WEEK 8 — 🏙️ My Community & Money

**Big idea:** **Diphthongs** (gliding vowel sounds) and the very 2nd-grade skills of **time & money** — anchored in a pretend town & store.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Read **diphthongs oi, oy, ou, ow** (coin, boy, out, cow).
- **Sight words:** *(Grade 1 → Grade 2)* would, very, your, put, please, thank.
- **Writing:** Write a short thank-you note (real audience!).
- **Math:** Tell time to the **hour & half-hour**; 🟦 to **5 minutes**; identify & 🟦 count **coins**.

**📚 Book basket:** *Last Stop on Market Street* · *The Berenstain Bears' Trouble with Money* · *Career Day* · *A Chair for My Mother* · *Curious George Takes a Job*.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | **oi / oy**: coin, soil / boy, toy. | Name coins (penny 1¢, nickel 5¢, dime 10¢, quarter 25¢). | Draw your neighborhood; label 3 places. | *Last Stop on Market Street*; notice helpers. |
| **Tue** | **ou / ow**: out, cloud / cow, owl. | Tell time to the **hour** on an analog clock. | "A helper in my town is a ___. They ___." | Community-helper dress-up / role play. |
| **Wed** | Diphthong mixed read; decodable. | Tell time to the **half-hour**; match clocks to digital. | Write a **thank-you note** to a real person. | Build a "town" map (roads, store, school). |
| **Thu** | Spelling with diphthongs (dictation: "boy," "out"). | 🟦 Count coins: how much is 2 dimes + 1 nickel? | Make price tags (5¢, 10¢) for the store. | **Pretend store** with real coins — buy & make change. |
| **Fri** | Review all vowel patterns so far (big sort). | 🟦 Time to 5 minutes; "what time do you…?" schedule. | Portfolio: the thank-you note + a "my day" timeline. | Map share; deliver the thank-you note for real. |

**Featured project:** A neighborhood map + a pretend store using real coins (money math you can touch).
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she tell time to the half-hour and name all four coins + values?

## WEEK 9 — 🌍 Animals Around the World

**Big idea:** **Word endings** (-s, -ed, -ing) & **syllables** (clap the parts) and **geometry + fractions** — shapes and "fair shares" everywhere in nature.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Add endings **-s, -ed, -ing**; clap & count **syllables**; read 2-syllable words.
- **Sight words:** *(Grade 2)* always, because, before, does, goes, right.
- **Writing:** Write 3–4 sentences (a mini report on an animal).
- **Math:** Name **2D & 3D shapes** and attributes (sides/corners/faces); partition into **halves & fourths**.

**📚 Book basket:** *The Mitten* · *Over and Under the Pond* · *National Geographic Little Kids: Animals* · *Actual Size* · *Who Would Win?* series (great 🟦 nonfiction).

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Read-aloud |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Plural **-s**: cat→cats, dog→dogs. | Name 2D shapes; count sides & corners. | Pick an animal to study; draw + label habitat. | *Actual Size*; compare animal sizes. |
| **Tue** | **-ing**: jump→jumping, read→reading. | Name 3D shapes (cube, sphere, cone, cylinder); find them at home. | Animal report sentence 1–2 (what it is, where it lives). | *Over and Under the Pond*; hidden habitats. |
| **Wed** | **-ed**: jump→jumped (notice the sounds). | 🟦 Sort shapes by attribute; build with blocks/clay. | Report sentence 3–4 (what it eats, a cool fact). | Diorama build (shoebox habitat). |
| **Thu** | **Syllables**: clap names & animal words (e‑le‑phant = 3). | **Fractions**: fold paper into **halves**; share a snack fairly. | Read the finished animal report aloud. | Finish diorama; present it. |
| **Fri** | Read a 2-syllable decodable; review endings. | 🟦 **Fourths**: fold into 4; "1 out of 4 parts." | Portfolio: animal report + a shapes-in-nature page. | *Who Would Win?* — predict & vote with reasons. |

**Featured project:** A shoebox **habitat diorama** + a 4-sentence animal report (her first "research").
🎯 **Checkpoint:** can she clap syllables and split a shape into equal halves & fourths?

## WEEK 10 — 🛠️ Maker Week & Academy Showcase  *(Final Check)*

**Big idea:** Consolidate everything, push one big 🟦 stretch (skip-counting → arrays → "groups of," the seed of multiplication), and **celebrate** with a showcase. This week is mostly review framed as a creative festival.
**Weekly objectives**
- **Phonics:** Review all patterns; read **multisyllable** words (sunset, rabbit, basket); fluency & expression.
- **Sight words:** review the full summer set; "words I can read" jar finale.
- **Writing:** Write a "What I learned this summer" page (4–5 sentences).
- **Math:** 🟦 **Skip-count by 2s, 5s, 10s**; build **arrays** ("3 rows of 4") and connect to repeated addition.
- **Run the [Final check](./assessment-checklists.md) and make a certificate.**

**📚 Book basket:** her **favorites from all 10 weeks** (let her choose the basket!) + *The Dot* and *Rosie Revere, Engineer* for maker spirit.

| Day | Reading & Phonics | Math | Journal / Writing | Theme & Project |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Multisyllable blending: sun‑set, rab‑bit, pic‑nic. | 🟦 Skip-count by **2s** (pairs of shoes, socks). | Brainstorm "What I learned" (mind-map). | *Rosie Revere, Engineer*; pick a maker project. |
| **Tue** | Fluency: reread a favorite with "storyteller voice." | 🟦 Skip-count by **5s** (hands) and **10s** (toes/dimes). | Draft the "What I learned" page (3 sentences). | Build the maker project (cardboard creation). |
| **Wed** | Reader's theater: act out an *Elephant & Piggie* book. | 🟦 **Arrays**: arrange counters in rows → "3 groups of 4 = 12." | Finish & illustrate the page (4–5 sentences). | Finish maker project; write its "instructions." |
| **Thu** | **Final phonics & reading check** (celebratory!). | **Final math check.** Then array bingo for fun. | Organize the **portfolio** start→finish together. | Set up the **Academy Showcase** (display the portfolio + projects). |
| **Fri** | She reads a book *to the family* (the big finale). | "Math museum": she teaches you one thing she learned. | Family writes notes in her portfolio. | 🎓 **SHOWCASE + certificate.** Compare baseline → final. Celebrate hugely! |

**Featured project:** The **Academy Showcase** — display the 10-week portfolio, projects, and growth journal; she presents; you award a certificate.
🎯 **Checkpoint:** **Final assessment.** Compare to baseline and mid-summer. Document the wins (this becomes her first "report" in the platform).

## After Week 10 — keeping momentum into the school year
- **Don't stop the nightly read-aloud.** Ever. It's the keystone habit.
- **Maintenance, not new ground:** 2–3× per week, 15 min — a few sight words, one math game, one journal sentence. Keeps skills warm without burning out.
- **Pick the next program:** "Beginning Reader Fluency," "Add & Subtract to 100," or "Write a Real Story." These become **Program 02, 03…** in Kaelyn's Academy — the agentic tutor will recommend the next one based on her final-check results.
